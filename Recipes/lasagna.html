Classic Beef Lasagna

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish that layers rich, savory flavors with creamy textures. This classic version features tender ground beef, a tangy tomato sauce, and layers of melted cheese, all stacked between sheets of pasta. It’s a comforting, hearty meal perfect for family dinners or gatherings. The dish is baked until bubbly and golden, creating a perfect balance of flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) spinach (optional)
  • 1 egg (optional, for ricotta mixture)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, half of the Parmesan cheese, and the egg (if using). Add spinach if desired.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of ricotta mixture, and then a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles topped with meat sauce and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
