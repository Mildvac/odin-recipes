Chicken Shawarma

Chicken shawarma is a Middle Eastern favorite that’s packed with bold, aromatic flavors. Marinated in a blend of garlic, lemon, and warm spices like cumin and paprika, the chicken is grilled or roasted to perfection. It’s traditionally served in pita bread with fresh vegetables, creamy tahini sauce, and a squeeze of lemon. This dish is vibrant, flavorful, and perfect for a quick yet satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • Pita bread, for serving
  • Sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and parsley (for garnish)
  • Tahini sauce or garlic sauce

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, salt, pepper, and cinnamon (if using).
  2. Add the chicken to the marinade, ensuring it is fully coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) or heat a grill to medium-high.
  4. Cook the chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice it into thin strips.
  6. Serve the chicken in warm pita bread with sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, parsley, and a drizzle of tahini or garlic sauce.
